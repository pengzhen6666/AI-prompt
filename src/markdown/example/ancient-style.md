## 古风意境专项视觉指南 (Ancient Style Vision Guide)

这是针对“古风意境”风格的专项视觉补充指令。请在保持基础模型（如 Lumina 4）的人物比例与光影实感的物理前提下，深度融合以下特征：

### 1. 发髻造型 (Traditional Hairstyling)
- **拒绝垂直自然**：强调 `elaborate traditional high buns (云髻)`, `intricate winding hair loops`, `voluminous updo with high crane-style hairstyle`, `sculptural hair styling`.
- **动态灵动词**：加入 `drifting baby hair strands`, `cascading messy hair pieces for atmospheric realism`.

### 2. 服饰材质 (Oriental Textile & Costume)
- **顶级织物层级**：`heavy royal silk`, `intricate gold-thread embroidery`, `layered translucent organza`, `shimmering brocade with depth`.
- **东方动感词**：`flowing wide sleeves (大袖)`, `fluttering silk ribbons (锦绣绶带)`, `billowing hemline`.

### 3. 环境与意向 (Symbolic Environment)
- **古典空间**：`ornate cinnabar red palace walls`, `traditional wooden lattice window screens`, ` Jiangnan garden moon gate frame`.
- **时令氛围词**：`drifting plum petals`, `misty incense smoke`, `shadowy bamboo silhouette`, `ethereal mountain fog`.

### 4. 视觉滤镜与调色 (Artistic Filter & Color)
- **色调倾向**：`muted elegant palette`, `traditional Chinese ink-wash aesthetic colors`, `warm amber highlight vs cool teal shadow`.
- **艺术渲染**：`painterly texture`, `soft focus filter`, `cinematic Tyndall effect`, `shimmering reflections on porcelain skin`.

### 5. 提示词融合建议 (Prompt Integration)
在构建 `core_prompt` 时，将上述词汇穿插在【主体词】与【光影词】阶段，确保发型与饰品（如 `jingling gold Bu-yao hairpins`, `dangling pearl bead strings`）被重点解析。
